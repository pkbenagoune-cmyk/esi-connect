How it works

Three simple steps to start learning or helping on ESI-Connect.

1

Create a request

A student describes their problem, selects the subject and difficulty level, then submits a help request on the platform.

2

A tutor accepts

A tutor browses the open requests and picks one they feel confident about. The request status changes to accepted.

3

Session completed

After the tutoring session, the request is marked as completed. Both students can rate the experience and continue learning.
